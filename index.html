ℹ️ About Google Sheets Sync

Clicking "Sync to Google Sheets" will send your current data to your connected Google Sheets spreadsheet. This creates a cloud backup that you can access from anywhere.

What gets synced: All sessions, clients, coaches, courts, packages, memberships, expenses, and client package assignments.

Sync frequency: Click the button whenever you want to backup your data to the cloud. For best results, sync regularly after making important changes.
